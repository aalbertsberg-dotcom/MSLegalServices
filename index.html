What We Do

We Are Your Legal Liaison

Legal Liaison
[ lee-guhl lee-AY-zahn ]
(n.) A legal liaison helps individuals or organizations understand legal processes, organize information, and communicate effectively with attorneys, courts, or agencies — while not giving legal advice or representing anyone in court.

Mississippi Legal Service delivers trusted, affordable legal support for individuals navigating legal matters on their own. Whether you're overwhelmed by paperwork, confused by deadlines, or unsure of next steps — our team is here to walk alongside you.

✦
We stop the ChatGPT spirals, the deadline panic, and the stress from confusing forms — so you can move forward with confidence.

Legal Liaison Services
